## Practice Questions:

### Easy Questions:

1. What is 30 % of 80? Yaha par ye soche ki question mark ke jagah par kya aayega.

Solution:

30 % of 80

= 30/100 × 80

= (30 × 80)/100

= 2400/100

= 24
