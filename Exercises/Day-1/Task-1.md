### Reading Material
**10 / 3 = 3** hota hai `/` *divide* ka sign hota hai.

**10 % 3 = 1** hota hai `%` modulas bolte hai joh ki ek number `10` agar hum `3`
se divide kare toh reminder kya ayega iska yeh bata ta hai.
